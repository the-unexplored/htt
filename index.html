Luxurious Stay at Heritage Tea Bungalows

Enjoy your stay at the luxurious tea bungalows located in serene settings surrounded by lush greenery.

Embrace the peace and take time to relax, rejuvenate, and connect with nature.

Tea Workshop

Participate in interactive workshops by passionate tea masters where you’ll learn to brew the perfect cup of tea. Uncover the secrets behind tea blending, explore the benefits of herbal infusions, and take home newfound knowledge to elevate your tea-drinking experience.

Tea Tasting Extravaganza

Engage your senses in a tea tasting extravaganza. Savour a wide range of teas, from delicate white and green teas to rich black and oolong varieties. Discover the subtle nuances, aromas, and flavours that make each tea unique.
